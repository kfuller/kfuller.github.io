The Premise
The missing layer in
every security program isn't technology.
it's performance.

Tools get bought and ignored. Frameworks get implemented and abandoned. The audit passes and nothing changes. The problem was never the software — it was who owns it, who understands it, and whether anyone cares enough to maintain it.

Security professionals are burning out inside programs that weren't built for humans. Manual everything, unclear ownership, zero recognition, no path forward. The talent is there. The operating system isn't.

RASOR Center operates at both levels. We coach the individual to perform at a higher level and advance faster. We advise the organization to build programs that actually run — with the right people, the right structure, and the right automation underneath it.
