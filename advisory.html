How It Works

The RASOR Engagement Model.

We lead every engagement at the architecture level. Our trained Fellows handle day-to-day operations. You get senior-level thinking without the overhead.

01
Strategy Call
30 minutes. We diagnose where your trust or risk program is costing you deals or slowing down your team. No pitch — just a real assessment of where the problem is.
02
Program Architecture
We scope the build engagement, define deliverables, and identify who inside your organization owns what. Clear start, clear end, clear outcome.
03
Build Phase
60–90 days. We build the program, document everything, and train your team. You get a fully operational Customer Trust or TPRM program with an internal owner who knows how to run it.

Once the program is live we stay engaged — managing operations, questionnaire flow, renewals, and reporting. When you're ready to bring it fully in-house, we place a trained RASOR Fellow to run it day to day.
